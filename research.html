Research & Publication

This project aims to bring together colour, texture, and storytelling – creating a growing archive of plant-inspired palettes.

Throughout Botanical Colour: The Redpath Way, three themes emerged from the research: Colour, Alchemy, and ‘All One’. These themes became the basis for a printed publication and a series of audio essays — designed to be experienced together.

As you turn through the pages of the publication, the audio guides you: fragments of oral histories recorded during the project, field recordings, music, and the voice of artist-researcher Abi Makepeace weaving context between them.

The publication and audio essays were created in collaboration by Abi Makepeace, Sam Bardsley, and Aurelia Lange – a process shaped by years of friendship and shared creative vision.

2000 copies of the publication were printed and distributed freely at the National Botanic Garden of Wales exhibition.

To experience the audio publication: Put on headphones. Open the publication to full screen. Allow 37 minutes duration.
