Botanical Colour - The Redpath way

Botanical Colour is a project exploring the natural relationship between plants and colour. Each swatch represents a unique shade inspired by the Redpath Archive and made by Makepeace Botanicals.

This project aims to bring together colour, texture, and storytelling – creating a growing archive of plant-inspired palettes.
